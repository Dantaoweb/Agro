About AgroCash

AgroCash connects investors, farmers, consultants, and other stakeholders in a decentralized agricultural ecosystem, providing transparent investment, productivity, and sustainability.

Features

  • Real-time tracking and updates for all stakeholders
  • Smart contracts and NFTs for project transparency and agreements
  • Validator and consultant onboarding with performance-based rewards
  • Secure, decentralized payments and fund distribution
  • Personalized dashboards and reports for each user role

User Access

Select your role to navigate to the respective dashboard:

  • Admin - Manage users, projects, and fund distributions
  • Farmer - Manage farm projects, submit business plans, track funding
  • Consultant - Quote charges, review plans, and monitor project progress
  • Validator - Validate tasks, earn rewards, and mint NFTs
  • Investor - View investment opportunities, track funding progress
  • Partner - Supplier or investor-partner roles, contributing services or capital
